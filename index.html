The Tangier Expedition

A day trip to Morocco is an adventure. Follow these key steps for a smooth journey across the Strait of Gibraltar.

  1. 1
    Bus to Tarifa

    Take an early bus from Malaga to the port town of Tarifa. This is crucial as its ferries arrive directly in Tangier's city center.

  2. 2
    Get Ferry Tickets

    Book online in advance. Arrive at the port 60-90 minutes early to exchange your e-ticket for a paper boarding pass.

  3. 3
    Onboard Passport Control

    This is the most important step. Find the Moroccan police onboard the ferry and get your passport stamped during the 1-hour crossing.

  4. 4
    Arrive in Tangier

    Disembark at Tangier Ville port. A pre-booked guide is highly recommended to maximize your time and avoid hassles.

September Packing Essentials
